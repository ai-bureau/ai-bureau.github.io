In 1967, IBM said: **“Machines should work. People should think.”** The boundary of what can be delegated to machines has shifted dramatically since then, but most people have not yet recognized it.

AI Bureau helps people see where routine work exists and hand it to machines.

## We do not take away work

We take away routine. People remain in the decision-making loop and retain responsibility.

## How we build AI systems

- We delegate to AI only what can be verified.
- We design the system and its constraints before implementation.
- We choose simple, understandable components over complexity for its own sake.
- We document every part of the system.

Automation is not the final goal. The more important question is: **what are you freeing your time for?**
