AI Bureau works with people and organizations that have **repeated processes with cognitive load** — processes they are used to calling work, although much of it is routine.

## Small businesses and entrepreneurs

Standard customer communication, documents, monitoring, and research. Every hour spent on operations is an hour not spent on growth.

## Volunteers and NGOs

Grants, reporting, logistics, and correspondence. People care deeply about their mission but drown in operations.

## Medium and large businesses

Integrating AI into business processes, making results transparent, and scaling without disproportionately expanding headcount.

## Freelancers and experts

Administrative routine consumes time that could be spent on core work, creativity, or rest.
